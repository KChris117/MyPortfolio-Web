BAT Assembly Engineering App

An internal web application for managing inventory and procurement (PR/PO) at the Assembly Engineering department.

🔒 Visit Live Website (Internal Only)

The Story Behind BAT Assembly App

Managing inventory and the procurement process for spare parts in a manufacturing environment is crucial to ensure smooth operations. Prior to this application, the tracking of stock and the approval workflow for Purchase Requisitions (PR) and Purchase Orders (PO) at the Assembly Engineering department were handled semi-manually via spreadsheets.

This manual process was vulnerable to data errors, loss of transaction history, and lengthy approval times. To address these issues, the BAT-Assembly Engineering App was developed. This web-based information system digitalizes the inventory tracking and multi-level approval workflows, drastically accelerating processing time and ensuring data accuracy.

This project was developed by Christoffel Aristo Marbun during an internship, aiming to improve operational efficiency through digital innovation.

Key Features

  • Inventory Management: Real-time tracking of spare part stocks with automatic deductions and additions based on transactions.
  • PR/PO Generation & Approval: Digitalized forms to create Purchase Requisitions and Purchase Orders with a multi-level approval workflow (User, Admin, Approver).
  • Master Data Configuration: Full control over master data including Cost Centers, GL Codes, and Categories.
  • Reporting (PDF Export): Dynamic generation of reports using the Rotativa library for easy physical archiving.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Secure access managed by ASP.NET Identity with encrypted logins.

Tech Stack

Framework: ASP.NET MVC (C#)

Database: SQL Server & Entity Framework (Code-First)

Frontend: Razor View Engine, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap

Reporting: Rotativa (PDF Generation)

Architecture: Model-View-Controller (MVC) & Waterfall Methodology
