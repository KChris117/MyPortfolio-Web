My Achievements

1st Place Art & Visualization - KMIPN VII 2025

A monumental milestone! As the team leader of PBL-IF-11, I, alongside my dedicated teammates Dewangga Fadillah Yusuf and Jamal Nur Agung, successfully secured 1st Place in the Art & Visualization category at KMIPN VII 2025. We competed with our project, Stock Rising, a digital investment board game. Guided by our incredible mentors Mr. Nanda Putra Perkasa and Mrs. Liony Lumombo, we faced sleepless nights, intense brainstorming, and relentless hard work to perfect the game's visuals. Winning this championship is a true testament to our team's resilience, creativity, and passion.
