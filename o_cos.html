The Story Behind O'Cos

O'Cos was born out of a challenge during the COVID-19 pandemic. We observed that many cosmetic businesses were struggling to sell their products offline due to social restrictions.

To overcome this hurdle and provide a modern solution, we built this exclusive e-commerce platform. O'Cos bridges the gap between premium cosmetic brands and beauty enthusiasts, ensuring that self-care and beauty can still thrive from the safety of home.

This project was proudly developed as a final project by Semester 1 Informatics Engineering students from Poltek Batam.

Key Features

  • Premium UI/UX: A modern, glassmorphism-inspired design optimized for Desktop and Mobile.
  • Product Catalog: Dynamic product grid with built-in pagination.
  • Authentication: Secure Login & Sign-up system with password hashing.
  • Shopping Cart: Add to cart, adjust quantities, and process simulated checkouts.
  • Wishlist: Users can easily save their favorite products.
  • Order Tracking: Monitor status (Pending, On Packing, On Delivery, Completed).

Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (Vanilla Custom CSS), JavaScript (Vanilla)

Backend: PHP 8 (Native / Procedural)

Database: MySQL (MariaDB)

Architecture: Server-Side Rendering (SSR) with multi-page structure.
