My Journey

A comprehensive timeline of my education, internships, competitions, and workshops.

2010 - 2016

SD Katolik Yos Sudarso Batam

Primary Education

I spent my formative years here as an active and curious student. Alongside my passion for playing football with friends, I developed an early interest in analytical thinking through strategy games like chess. This period also marked my first introduction to digital literacy, where I learned the fundamentals of computer software such as MS Word, Excel, and Paint.

2016 - 2019

SMP Negeri 12 Batam

Junior High School

Continuing my education at SMPN 12 Batam, I began to refine my communication and public speaking skills. Through frequent school projects and class assignments, I became highly proficient in using MS PowerPoint to create engaging presentations. This experience was crucial in building my confidence to present ideas clearly in front of my peers.

2019 - 2022

SMA Negeri 20 Batam

Science Major (IPA)

My high school years were incredibly dynamic. In my first year, I proudly participated in the national Christian choir competition, Pesparawi. When the global pandemic shifted our learning entirely online via Google Classroom, I adapted quickly. I maintained academic excellence with an average score above 88, earning consecutive academic achievement awards from my church community and the Batak cultural association (Bona Taon).

2023 - Present

Politeknik Negeri Batam

Diploma in Informatics Engineering

After taking a dedicated gap year to prepare for higher education, I enrolled in the Informatics Engineering program. Instead of traditional student organizations, I focused heavily on practical software development through the campus's intensive Project-Based Learning (PBL) curriculum every semester. This hands-on approach culminated in my team winning 1st Place at KMIPN VII. I am currently finalizing my graduation requirements, anticipating my graduation ceremony in October 2026.

October 13, 2025

KMIPN VII 2025 - Project Presentation

Team Leader (PBL-IF-11)

As the team leader of PBL-IF-11, alongside my incredible members Dewangga Fadillah Yusuf and Jamal Nur Agung, we presented our project, Stock Rising, to the judges of the 7th National Student Exhibition of Informatics Polytechnic (KMIPN VII). We competed in the Game Application Development category via an online Zoom evaluation. Stock Rising is an Android-based multiplayer digital board game simulating stock investments, complete with dynamic market cycles. We were heavily guided by our supervisor, Ir. Liony Lumombo, S.ST., M.IDes, IPM.

October 15, 2025

Winner Announcement in Padang

1st Place - Art & Visualization

The highly anticipated winner announcement took place in Padang. Even though our category was held online, representatives from Politeknik Negeri Batam attended the event in person and captured the moment our team was announced as the 1st Place Winner for Art & Visualization in the Game Application Development category. It was an incredibly proud moment for us and our campus.

October 22, 2025

Official Certificate Received

Recognizing Our Achievement

We officially received our winning certificates from the KMIPN VII committee. This document formally recognizes our hard work in developing Stock Rising, particularly in crafting the interactive UI, 3D assets, and engaging visual mechanics that won us the Art & Visualization award against many other talented teams nationwide.

December 12, 2025

Awarding Night at Poltek Batam

Campus Appreciation & Celebration

Politeknik Negeri Batam hosted an Awarding Night at the campus auditorium to honor students who achieved national victories. I had the honor of receiving our award directly from the Director of Politeknik Negeri Batam, Bambang Hendrawan. It was a memorable night filled with celebrations alongside other talented winning teams from our campus.

December 19, 2025

Project Completion & Final Celebration

Gratitude to Our Mentors

We concluded our journey by taking a memorable group photo with our supervisor, Ir. Liony Lumombo, S.ST., M.IDes, IPM., and our Project Manager, Nanda Putra Perkasa S.Tr.Kom. Their relentless guidance, feedback, and support were instrumental in transforming Stock Rising from an idea into a national award-winning game.
